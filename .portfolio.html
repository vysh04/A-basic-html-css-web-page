Preventing the spread of COVID-19

Wear a mask in public indoor spaces in areas with widespread transmission of the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Avoid close contact with people who are sick. Minimize touching your eyes, nose, and mouth. Stay home when you are sick. Cover your cough or sneeze with a tissue, then throw the tissue in the trash. Clean frequently touched objects and surfaces regularly.Wash your hands often with soap and water.
